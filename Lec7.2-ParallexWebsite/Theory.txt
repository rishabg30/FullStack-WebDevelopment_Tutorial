## Parallex Effect ##

What is Parallex Effect ?
Imagine you are sitting in a car and car is moving. You see buildings from your car window and a moon.
You discover that buildings are moving fast but moon is moving slow wrt to your car movement.
This is because buildings are at compartively much lesser distance to you than moon.
This effect is know as Parallex effect. {3D effect}. 
    Building acts as a foreground image.
    Mooom acts as a background image.
